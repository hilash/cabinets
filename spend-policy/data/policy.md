# Employee Spend Policy v2.4
**Effective: February 1, 2026**

This policy applies to all company-issued Ramp and Brex cardholders and to all expense reimbursement requests.

---

## Section 1 — General Principles

1.1 Spend company money as you would spend your own. If you would not buy it personally, you probably should not buy it on the company card.

1.2 All business expenses must have a clear, legitimate business purpose. Personal expenses are never reimbursable.

1.3 Receipts are required for all transactions over $25.00. Transactions without receipts will be flagged for follow-up.

1.4 Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the transaction date. Submissions older than 60 days require VP approval.

---

## Section 2 — Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approval Required |
|---|---|
| Under $500 | No pre-approval needed; policy compliance required |
| $500–$2,500 | Manager approval before purchase |
| $2,500–$10,000 | Director/VP approval before purchase |
| Over $10,000 | CFO approval before purchase |
| Software subscriptions (any amount) | IT + Finance approval before purchase |

---

## Section 3 — Meals & Entertainment

3.1 **Team meals (internal only):** Up to $40 per person. Attendee list required for 3+ people.

3.2 **Client meals (external guests present):** Up to $125 per person. Guest names and company required.

3.3 **Working lunch/solo meal:** Up to $25 per person, maximum 3 times per week.

3.4 **Team celebrations:** Up to $75 per person. Requires manager approval and a minimum of 3 attendees.

3.5 **Alcohol:** Reimbursable as part of a client meal (Section 3.2 limits apply). Not reimbursable as a standalone expense.

3.6 **Coffee meetings:** Up to $15 per person. No attendee list required under 4 people.

---

## Section 4 — Travel

4.1 **Flights:** Economy class for all flights under 6 hours. Business class allowed for flights over 6 hours with VP approval. Book at least 14 days in advance unless urgent (< 72 hours notice).

4.2 **Hotels:** Up to $250/night in standard markets; up to $350/night in high-cost markets (NYC, SF, London, Zurich). Use the corporate rate (Marriott Bonvoy ID: CORP-34821).

4.3 **Ground transport:** Uber/Lyft to/from airport are reimbursable. Black car or Uber Black requires manager approval. Personal car mileage at IRS rate (currently $0.67/mile).

4.4 **Per diem:** No separate per diem — use the meal limits in Section 3 for travel days.

4.5 **Conference registration:** Reimbursable with manager approval. Registration over $1,500 requires Director approval.

---

## Section 5 — Software & Subscriptions

5.1 All software purchases require IT and Finance approval before purchase, regardless of amount. This includes free trials with payment info.

5.2 Personal productivity tools (e.g., Notion Personal, 1Password Personal) are not reimbursable. Company-wide licenses are managed by IT.

5.3 AI tools and APIs: Covered on a case-by-case basis with Engineering/IT approval. Personal ChatGPT Plus subscriptions are not reimbursable.

---

## Section 6 — Office Supplies & Equipment

6.1 **Office supplies:** Up to $50/month without approval. Receipts required over $25.

6.2 **Home office equipment (remote employees):** Up to $500 per year for peripherals (keyboard, monitor, headset). Laptop, dock, and primary monitor are provided by IT — do not purchase separately.

6.3 **Books & learning:** Up to $200/year for books, courses, or certifications. Additional amounts require manager approval.

---

## Section 7 — Excluded Categories

The following are never reimbursable, regardless of amount or approval:

- Personal clothing, grooming, or personal care
- Gym memberships or fitness equipment (exceptions: on-site corporate gym or specific role-based fitness allowance)
- Fines, traffic tickets, or parking violations
- Political donations or contributions
- Personal subscriptions (streaming, news, music)
- Gifts to employees over $50 (requires HR + Finance approval above this threshold)
- Cash advances

---

## Section 8 — Violation Consequences

8.1 Policy violations will result in the expense being rejected and the employee responsible for personal reimbursement.

8.2 Repeated violations (3+ in a rolling 12-month period) will result in card suspension and an HR review.

8.3 Fraudulent expense submission is grounds for immediate termination.

## 🌿 Natural events worth chasing

| Event | Where | When | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cherry blossom peak bloom** | Washington, DC | Late March-early April | Tidal Basin in peak is unreal |
| **Synchronous fireflies** | Great Smoky Mountains, TN | Late May-early June | Lottery-only access; once-in-a-lifetime |
| **California superbloom** | SoCal deserts | March-April after wet winters | Doesn't happen every year — when it does, it's biblical |
| **Monarch butterfly migration** | Pacific Grove, CA + Pismo Beach, CA | November-February | Tens of thousands clustered on eucalyptus |
| **Salmon run** | Brooks Falls, Katmai, AK | Mid-July | Bears + salmon. The famous photo |
| **Dark sky / Milky Way** | Cherry Springs, PA / Big Bend, TX | New moons, summer | Best stargazing in lower 48 |
| **Peak fall foliage** | New England (early-mid Oct), Smokies (mid-Oct), Aspens in CO (late Sep) | Oct | The road trip |
| **Total solar eclipse** | Path varies | Whenever | Plan early; hotels in the path sell out years out |

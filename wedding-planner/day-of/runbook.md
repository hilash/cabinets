# ⏱️ Master Timeline (Runbook)

> The bootstrap drafts a starter version of this from your vendor schedule. The Planner agent updates it as vendor times change. Final version is locked at the rehearsal — no changes after that.

## How to use this file

- The bootstrap fills in a structural draft based on your ceremony time and reception style.
- As vendors confirm specific times, update the table.
- Run [[../generators/day-of-timeline-generator]] one final time the week before — that produces the per-vendor briefs to send out.

## Who has authority on the day

| Decision | Who calls it |
|---|---|
| Weather pivot (indoor / outdoor) | Couple — by T-3h, decision is final |
| Timeline adjustments | Day-of coordinator (or designated point person) |
| Speech cuts (running long) | MC or designated speech-keeper |
| Vendor disputes | Day-of coordinator — couple is shielded |
| Guest issues | Wedding-party point person |
| Last-minute family drama | Designated family wrangler — couple is shielded |

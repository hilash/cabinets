# 🏛️ Kajabi

> All-in-one platform: courses + email + sales pages + memberships + coaching + community. Expensive but covers everything.

## Pick this if

- You're building a real BUSINESS around teaching (not just one course)
- You want courses + email + funnels + memberships in one tool
- You're past your first course and willing to invest in infrastructure
- You like an opinionated, full-stack tool

## Don't pick this if

- This is your first course and you just want to ship
- You're price-sensitive — $149+/mo is the floor
- You already have a good email tool (ConvertKit / Beehiiv) you don't want to leave

## Pricing (verify)

- Basic: ~$149/mo
- Growth: ~$199/mo
- Pro: ~$399/mo

The price reflects what's included. If you'd otherwise pay for ConvertKit ($30) + Teachable ($40) + a sales page builder ($30) + a community tool ($50), you're already at $150 — Kajabi consolidates that.

## What Kajabi does that smaller tools don't

- **Native email + automation.** Drip sequences, segments, broadcasts — comparable to ConvertKit.
- **Funnel builder.** Multi-step funnels with upsells, order bumps, downsells.
- **Memberships.** Sell access to a library of content as a recurring product.
- **Coaching products.** Bookable 1-on-1 calls with payment built in.
- **Community.** Built-in member communities (replaced their old "Circle" integration).
- **Mobile app.** Branded, white-label app for your students.
- **Analytics.** Real revenue analytics, cohort views, etc.

## What Kajabi doesn't do well

- Live cohort scheduling — basic at best, weaker than Maven
- Open-source / self-hosted feel — it's a SaaS through and through
- Cheap — the price is the price

## Recommended setup

1. Sign up for the lowest tier. Use the 14-day trial.
2. Build ONE course first. Resist the urge to build the funnel + membership + coaching products on day one.
3. Use Kajabi's pre-built templates for sales pages — they convert fine and save days.
4. Migrate your email list in. Set up a welcome sequence.
5. Connect Stripe. Set up tax handling for your jurisdiction.
6. Once the first course is shipped and a cohort or two has run, then consider memberships / coaching / funnels.

## Kajabi vs Teachable + ConvertKit

| | Kajabi | Teachable + ConvertKit |
|---|---|---|
| Total monthly | $149 | $40 + $30 = $70 |
| Email power | Strong | ConvertKit is stronger |
| Funnel power | Strong | Weak |
| Course hosting | Good | Good |
| Setup complexity | Medium | Low |
| Migration cost later | Hard (everything's tangled) | Easy (everything's separate) |

For a first course: Teachable + ConvertKit. For a real teaching business: Kajabi pays back the price.

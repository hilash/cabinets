# Helios  ·  Tier 1  ·  Threat: → Steady

> One-line: The slow incumbent. Strong brand and procurement gravity in enterprise; we win on velocity and modern UX.

> **⚠ Freshness:** Intel verified through **2026-05-16** (last Helios signal). Feeds dark since — no live sources wired (signal log 06-18). The "AI Assistant still a waitlist" line is the one to spot-check before leaning on it — see landmine.

## Positioning & ICP
- **Pitch:** "The trusted standard." Sells safety, references, and procurement ease to enterprise IT.
- **ICP:** 1,000+ seat enterprise, regulated industries, top-down buyer.

## Strengths
1. **Brand & references.** Procurement trusts them; incumbent advantage in RFPs.
2. **Breadth.** Huge feature surface; checks every RFP box.
3. **Services org.** White-glove implementation enterprises like.

## Weaknesses
1. **Velocity.** ~1 meaningful release per quarter; UI feels 2018.
2. **Time-to-value.** 90-day implementations are normal; we land in 2 weeks.
3. **Price uplifts + slipping support.** Annual uplifts of 8–12% cited in reviews; 3 new G2 reviews in May cite slow support on top of the uplift (2026-05-16). The "safe choice" is getting more expensive and less responsive at the same time.

## Battlecard (for a rep mid-call)
**When they say:** "Helios is the safe, proven choice."
**You say:** "Proven, yes — and slow. Their last meaningful release was a quarter ago and their AI work is still a waitlist. You'd buy the 2018 product at a 2026 price with a 90-day implementation. We land in two weeks and ship weekly. Let me show you the changelog side-by-side."

**Traps to set:** Ask for their last 3 shipped features with dates, their implementation timeline, and their guaranteed support response time — recent customers are publicly complaining about all three.
**Proof points:** Changelog velocity, 2-week onboarding case study, TCO incl. their annual uplift, May 2026 G2 reviews citing slow support (2026-05-16).
**Landmines (don't):**
- Don't attack their brand head-on with a champion who chose them before — reframe to velocity + TCO.
- **Don't claim "their AI is still just a waitlist" as current fact.** Last confirmed 2026-05-16; a GA could have landed in the 30-day feed gap. If the prospect is close to Helios, say "as of our last check" or spot-check the blog/changelog first — getting this wrong on a call hands them the velocity point.

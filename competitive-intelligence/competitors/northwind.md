# Northwind  ·  Tier 1  ·  Threat: ↑ Rising

> One-line: The fast-follower undercutting us in mid-market. Cheaper, shipping quickly, and just closed our security differentiation.

> **⚠ Freshness:** Intel verified through **2026-05-19**. Signal feeds have been dark since (no live sources wired — see signal log 06-18). Pricing/positioning below is our best current truth; the EU-residency claim is the one to spot-check before a deal — see landmine.

## Positioning & ICP
- **Pitch:** "Enterprise power without the enterprise price." Targets 50–500 seat mid-market.
- **ICP:** Ops and RevOps leaders at Series B–D SaaS. Strong in North America, weak in EU (no data residency).

## Strengths
1. **Price.** 20% under us at Pro after the May cut.
2. **Velocity.** Shipped 9 changelog items in 30 days vs our 5 (as of 2026-05-19; latest was Bulk import v2).
3. **Onboarding.** Self-serve, time-to-value < 1 day; we still gate on a CS call.

## Weaknesses
1. **No EU data residency.** We win every EU/regulated deal on this.
2. **Shallow reporting.** Their analytics is a single dashboard; we have the narrative layer.
3. **Support SLA.** G2 reviews repeatedly cite slow support (see signals 2026-05-14).

## Battlecard (for a rep mid-call)
**When they say:** "Northwind does the same thing for 20% less."
**You say:** "On Pro list price, yes. Two things: their SSO-on-Pro means your security team inherits a weaker isolation model — happy to walk your CISO through it. And there's no EU data residency, so if Frankfurt is on the roadmap you'd re-platform in a year. Here's the 3-year TCO including that switch."

**Traps to set:** Ask about EU data residency and support SLA early — both are documented weak spots.
**Proof points:** EU residency cert, 99.9% support SLA, the narrative-reporting demo.
**Landmines (don't):**
- Don't argue raw price — you lose. Pivot to TCO + risk.
- **Don't assert "no EU data residency" as a fresh fact.** It's our #1 win condition against Northwind and our last verified read is 05-19. They are hiring upmarket + added a Security/SOC2 tab — an EU-residency announcement is the open escalation thread (signal log 06-18) and our feed wouldn't currently catch it. Frame it as "as of our last check" and confirm live before betting the deal on it.

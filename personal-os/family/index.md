# Family

The **people** side of the household. Kids, partner, extended family. Everything about humans and their schedules.

Money and maintenance live in `home/` and `money/`. Keep this one about people.

## What lives here

- `members.md` — everyone in the household. Names, ages, allergies, pediatrician, school.
- `calendar/` — week-ahead views, activity schedules, carpool plans.
- `school/` — permission slips, deadlines, teacher contacts, forms.
- `babysitters.md` — roster with availability, rates, preferred kids, notes.
- `birthdays.md` — everyone the family remembers. With gift ideas kept fresh.
- `briefs/` — weekly family brief.

## Team

- **Family Coordinator** — the one-page "here's the week" brief. Friday afternoon.
- **School Liaison** — deadlines, forms, teacher emails, permission slips. Never misses a field trip.

## Weekly rhythm

- **Friday 4pm** — Family Coordinator writes the week-ahead brief. Who goes where with what. What the partner needs to handle.
- **Friday 5pm** — Babysitter confirmations go out for next week's slots.
- **Sunday** — School Liaison scans the inbox for anything school-related arriving Monday.

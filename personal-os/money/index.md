# Money

Budget, subscriptions, taxes, net worth. This cabinet answers two questions:

- **Where did the money go last month?**
- **Where should it go next month?**

## What lives here

- `budget.md` — monthly budget by category, with targets and actuals.
- `transactions/` — monthly CSV exports from bank/credit cards.
- `subscriptions.md` — every recurring charge. What it costs. Last-used date. Keep/cut status.
- `goals.md` — savings goals. Amount, deadline, current progress.
- `networth.md` — monthly snapshot of accounts + debts.
- `taxes/` — annual folder for receipts, forms, deductions.
- `briefs/` — monthly money brief.

## Team

- **Budget Keeper** — monthly brief explaining where it went and what to change. Not a scold. A friend with a spreadsheet.
- **Subscription Auditor** — quarterly sweep of every recurring charge. "You paid $179 for something you used twice in 6 months."

## Monthly rhythm

- **3rd of each month** — Budget Keeper runs the prior-month brief once transactions have cleared.
- **1st of the quarter** — Subscription Auditor produces the cut list.

## Cross-department

- Bills Clerk (in `home/`) keeps recurring bills current — mirror their list here.
- Health cabinet informs: out-of-pocket medical goes here.
- Family cabinet informs: activity fees, babysitter rates, birthday gift budgets.

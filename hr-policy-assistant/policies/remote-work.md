# Remote Work & Hybrid Policy

**Effective:** February 1, 2026 · **Last reviewed:** February 28, 2026 · **Status:** Aging (review recommended)

## Summary

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. At least 2 in-office days per week are expected for roles designated Hybrid. Fully remote roles are designated at hire.

## Key rules

- Remote work is a privilege, not a right, and can be revoked with 2 weeks' notice.
- Home office equipment budget: $500 one-time allowance for remote setup. Receipts required. See `policies/expenses.md`.
- Employees must be available during core hours (10:00–16:00 local time, Mon–Fri).
- Travel to the office for quarterly all-hands is required for all employees, including fully remote.
- International remote work (outside country of employment) requires prior approval from HR and Legal.

## Edge cases

- **Fully remote employees:** Quarterly travel to the nearest hub is expensed by the company.
- **Manager discretion:** Managers can require more in-office days for performance or project reasons with 1-week notice.

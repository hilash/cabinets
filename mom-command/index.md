# 💛 Mom Command

> **The always-installed root for the Mom & Baby cabinet series.** Holds the shared context — your bio, the baby profile, every log, every appointment. Stage cabinets (newborn, infant, crawler...) plug into this and read from it.

## Open these first

- 📊 [[mission-control/index|Mission Control]] — full-screen dashboard. Last feed, last sleep, last diaper, baby's age, next milestone, growth percentile, next appointment.
- 📝 [[bio|Who you are, what you want]] — your story, the baby's story, your partner's role, what kind of parent you want to be. Read by Martha to calibrate everything she says back.
- 👶 [[baby-profile|Baby profile]] — name, DOB, weight/length history, allergies, birth details
- 🏥 [[pediatrician|Pediatrician info]] · [[emergency-contacts|Emergency contacts]]

## The logs (Martha reads these every morning)

- 🍼 [[logs/feed-log|Feed log]]
- 😴 [[logs/sleep-log|Sleep log]]
- 💩 [[logs/diaper-log|Diaper log]] *(your pediatrician will ask)*
- 🌟 [[logs/milestone-log|Milestone log]]
- 📈 [[logs/growth-chart|Growth chart]] *(WHO percentiles, plotted)*
- 💉 [[logs/vaccination-schedule|Vaccination schedule]] *(CDC schedule, dates done, next due)*

## Your team — 1 agent

- 💛 **Martha, your chief of staff** — knows every cabinet you have installed, every log entry, what the pediatrician said last visit, who's due for a vaccine. Warm, funny, a little sarcastic. Texts at 3am. Says "call your doctor" when she needs to. **The only agent across every cabinet in this series** — her title shifts per stage but her voice doesn't.

## Stage cabinets (install one at a time)

| Stage | Cabinet | Window |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-conception | `trying-to-conceive` | 0–12+ months |
| First trimester | `pregnancy-trimester-1` | weeks 1–13 |
| Second trimester | `pregnancy-trimester-2` | weeks 14–27 |
| Third trimester | `pregnancy-trimester-3` | weeks 28–40+ |
| Birth prep | `birth-plan` | one-time |
| Mom recovery | `fourth-trimester` | weeks 0–12 postpartum |
| **Newborn** | `newborn` | **0–3 months** ← *the survival cabinet* |
| Infant | `infant` | 3–6 months |
| Crawler | `crawler` | 6–9 months |
| Pre-walker | `pre-walker` | 9–12 months |
| Early toddler | `early-toddler` | 12–18 months |
| Toddler | `toddler` | 18–24 months |
| Two-year-old | `two-year-old` | 24–36 months |

You graduate from one cabinet to the next. Old cabinets stay browsable as the archive of completed milestones + captured memories.

## Daily rhythm

- **6:00 am** — Martha drops `morning-brief` into your inbox: today's feed goal, one milestone to notice, one self-care reminder, today's appointments
- **Sunday 8:00 pm** — Martha generates `weekly-roundup-pdf`: week's feeds/sleep/wins, photo, next week preview

## Why everything is in CSVs

CSVs open as interactive table editors in Cabinet — you can log a feed in 2 taps. They also roundtrip through git, sync across devices, and feed agent prompts directly. No proprietary databases, no vendor lock-in. If you ever want out, your data is just files.

⚠ diverging — lower the learning rate

Small LR is slow but safe. Big LR can diverge — the ball overshoots and flies off a cliff. Momentum helps in long narrow valleys (try the valley landscape). In a real neural net, the landscape has millions of dimensions, but your intuition from this 2D toy will take you remarkably far.

Read one research paper critically

Closing the loop · earn the right to skim arXiv
Pick one influential paper (Attention Is All You Need is the canonical choice), read it line-by-line, write a 1-page summary in your own words. After this you'll know whether a new paper is worth your weekend.
1 hour
read + write
Recommended paper: Attention Is All You Need (Vaswani et al., 2017) — the original transformer paper. Read on arXiv. Write your own one-pager covering: what problem does it solve, what's the key idea, what's the experimental result, what would you do next.

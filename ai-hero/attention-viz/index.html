What just happened

The attention formula
attention(Q, K, V) = softmax(Q·Kᵀ / √d_k) · V

In plain English
For each token, compute a similarity score with every other token. Softmax those into weights. Use the weights to take a weighted average of value vectors. That weighted average is the new representation.

This is it. Attention is a weighted sum over values, where the weights come from the input itself. Stack many heads in parallel, stack many such blocks in sequence, and you have a transformer. Everything else — layernorm, residuals, MLP blocks — is supporting infrastructure around this core idea.

# Evaluations

Every job offer gets a 6-block evaluation before you spend time tailoring a CV or applying. This is the quality gate.

## The 6-Block System

| Block | Weight | What It Covers |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A** Role Summary | 20% | Scope, team, growth potential, red flags |
| **B** CV Match | 25% | Skills overlap, gaps, ATS keywords |
| **C** Level Strategy | 15% | Seniority fit, trajectory, inflation check |
| **D** Compensation | 15% | Market benchmarks, total comp, equity |
| **E** Personalization | 10% | Culture signals, application angle |
| **F** Interview Prep | 15% | Likely questions, STAR+R mapping |

## Score Thresholds

| Score | Recommendation | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **4.0+** | Strong Apply | Tailor CV immediately. Prioritize warm path. |
| **3.5-3.9** | Consider | Apply only if pipeline needs volume or warm referral exists. |
| **3.0-3.4** | Weak | Skip unless strategic (learning a new domain, target company). |
| **Below 3.0** | Skip | Move to Withdrawn. Your time is better spent elsewhere. |

## Saved Evaluations

Each completed evaluation is saved as a page here, named `eval-[company]-[role-slug].md`. The page contains all six blocks with scores, the weighted total, and the go/no-go recommendation.

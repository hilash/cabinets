PM for Supabase's developer tools. Supabase is an open-source Firebase alternative — Postgres-backed BaaS with auth, storage, edge functions, and real-time subscriptions. The "Developer Tools" PM likely owns the SDK ecosystem, CLI experience, local development workflow, and/or the dashboard. This is a high-autonomy role at a company that moves fast and ships loudly.

**Team size / reporting:**
Supabase has ~150 employees (2026 estimate). Product team is small — likely 2–4 PMs total. Reporting to VP Product or a product lead. High individual ownership; engineers ship themselves.

**Growth potential (2–3 years):**
PM → Senior PM → PM Lead within a fast-growing startup. Supabase is positioned for an IPO or Series C, which could accelerate the timeline. However, this is an IC role — no management scope implied by the title.

**Red flags:**

- Supabase ships extremely fast with high community pressure. Could be exhausting if the pace preference is more deliberate.

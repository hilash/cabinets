# CV Lab

Every application gets its own tailored CV, generated by the CV Tailor agent from your master CV and the Evaluator's Block B analysis.

## How It Works

1. A role scores 4.0+ in evaluation
2. CV Tailor reads the job posting and Block B (CV Match Analysis)
3. Extracts keywords, required skills, and cultural signals
4. Selects the strongest proof points from `/profile/proof-points.md`
5. Rewrites and reorders the master CV to maximize match
6. Saves the tailored CV here as `cv-[company]-[role-slug].md`

## Naming Convention

```
cv-streamai-pm-ai-platform.md
cv-datahub-senior-pm-hub.md
cv-flowhq-head-of-product.md
```

## ATS Rules (enforced by CV Tailor)

- Standard section headers only (Summary, Experience, Education, Skills)
- No tables, columns, or graphics
- Both acronyms and full forms included
- Keywords from the posting in the top third
- Max 40% changed from master CV — you should still sound like yourself
- One page for <10 years experience

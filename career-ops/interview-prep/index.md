# Interview Prep

The Interview Strategist works from Block F evaluations to build company-specific prep packets. Everything lives here.

## What Lives Here

- **[[story-bank]]** — STAR+R stories mapped to competencies. The reusable ammo.
- **[[negotiation-scripts]]** — Scripts for counters, geographic pushback, competing offers, equity, and timing.
- Company-specific prep packets (generated per interview)

## The STAR+R Difference

Classic STAR tells what happened. STAR+**R** (Reflection) tells what you learned. The Reflection is what makes an interviewer think "this person is self-aware and still growing."

| Component | Time | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Situation | 10 sec | Set the scene |
| Task | 10 sec | Your specific job |
| Action | 40 sec | What YOU did (not your team) |
| Result | 15 sec | Quantified outcome |
| Reflection | 15 sec | What you'd do differently or learned |

**Total: 90 seconds.** Practice with a timer.

## Company-Specific Prep

For each interview, the Interview Strategist generates:
1. Company context (recent news, product direction, team structure)
2. Role-specific questions based on Block F analysis
3. 3-5 STAR+R stories mapped to the likely question themes
4. 2-3 questions to ask them (that show you've done your homework)
5. Red flags to probe (scope clarity, manager quality, team health)

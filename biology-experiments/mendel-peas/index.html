About the experiment

Gregor Mendel crossed pea plants with contrasting traits, then counted offspring types across generations. Dominant alleles such as round seed shape or yellow seed color can mask recessive alleles in F1 plants.

When F1 hybrids self-pollinate, hidden recessive alleles can reappear in F2. The ratio bars here reduce the Punnett square counts to the simplest whole-number pattern.

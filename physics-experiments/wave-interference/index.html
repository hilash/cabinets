Wave Interference

Two coherent point sources emit circular waves. Where crests meet crests, amplitude doubles (constructive); where crest meets trough, they cancel (destructive). The hyperbolic fringes you see are loci of constant path difference.
Drag the sources to reposition them.
Ripple mode shows instantaneous displacement. Intensity mode shows time-averaged |ψ|² — the pattern a long-exposure camera would capture.

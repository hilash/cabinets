# Real Estate Brokerage

A real estate brokerage managing property listings, marketing campaigns, and client relationships. The operation spans from lead generation through closing, with specialized teams for each phase.

## Why This Template

- Shows a services business with distinct operational phases: listings, marketing, client relations.
- Natural fit for local market data and time-sensitive inventory management.
- Demonstrates how industry-specific workflows map to the cabinet model.

## Key Assets

- [[market-data]] for local market trends, pricing, and competitive analysis
- [[listing-templates]] for property listing copywriting and photography guidelines

## Active Root Team

- Broker for deal oversight, pricing strategy, and agent coordination
- Operations Manager for transaction pipeline, compliance, and process efficiency

## Child Cabinets

- `listings` for active property inventory, pricing analysis, and listing quality
- `marketing` for property marketing campaigns, open house coordination, and lead generation
- `client-relations` for buyer/seller communication, follow-ups, and relationship nurturing

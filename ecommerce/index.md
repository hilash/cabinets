# E-commerce Store

A direct-to-consumer brand selling physical products online. The operation covers product sourcing, inventory management, email-driven retention marketing, and order fulfillment.

## Why This Template

- Covers the core DTC loop: source products, drive traffic, convert, fulfill, retain.
- Natural separation between operations (inventory, fulfillment) and marketing (email, promotions).
- Shows how a lean team can automate repetitive tasks like inventory checks and promo scheduling.

## Company Modules

- [[company/strategy]]
- [[company/catalog]]

## Active Root Team

- CEO for brand strategy, vendor relationships, and margin targets
- Head of Ops for inventory levels, supplier coordination, and fulfillment quality

## Child Cabinets

- `inventory` for stock monitoring, reorder triggers, and supplier communication
- `marketing/email` for lifecycle campaigns, promotional calendars, and segmentation
- `fulfillment` for shipping operations, returns processing, and delivery tracking

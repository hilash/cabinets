# IT Helpdesk FAQ

Common questions answered here are deflected from the ticket queue automatically.

---

## Q: How do I connect to VPN?

Install the Cisco AnyConnect client from the Self-Service portal (macOS) or Software Center (Windows). Sign in with your corporate email and your Okta MFA. VPN server: `vpn.corp.example.com`. If you get an error after MFA, clear your browser cookies and try again. If still failing after 2 attempts, submit a ticket.

---

## Q: How do I request software / add a new app?

Submit a software request via this cabinet's #it-help channel. Include: app name, business justification, estimated number of users, and whether a free trial exists. IT evaluates security review, licensing cost, and SSO compatibility. Standard approval time: 3–5 business days.

---

## Q: My laptop is running slowly — what should I do?

1. Restart your machine (not sleep — full restart).
2. Check available disk space: you need at least 15 GB free for normal performance.
3. Check for pending macOS/Windows updates in System Settings / Windows Update.
4. Open Activity Monitor (Mac) or Task Manager (Windows) and look for processes using >50% CPU.
If none of the above helps, submit a P3 hardware ticket and IT will schedule a remote diagnostic.

---

## Q: How do I get admin rights on my machine?

Admin rights are not granted to individual users by default. Submit an access request with business justification. IT Security reviews all admin requests — expected turnaround 2 business days. Contractors are not eligible for admin rights.

---

## Q: My Okta / SSO session keeps logging me out — what's wrong?

Okta sessions expire after 8 hours of inactivity (12 hours for mobile). If you're getting logged out more frequently, clear your browser cache and disable any VPN browser extensions that may interfere with session cookies. If the issue persists, submit a P3 Account ticket.

---

## Q: How do I set up a new hire's laptop before their start date?

Submit a new hire setup ticket at least 5 business days before the start date. Include: hire's name, start date, department, manager, and whether they need Mac or Windows. IT will provision the machine, install standard software, and have it ready for day one.

---

## Q: How do I report a phishing email?

Forward the email as an attachment to `phishing@corp.example.com`. Do not click any links or download attachments in the suspected phishing email. If you think you already clicked a link, submit a P1 Security ticket immediately and include the URL you visited.

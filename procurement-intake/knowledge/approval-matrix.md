# Approval Authority Matrix

| Annual Cost | Required Approvers | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Under $5,000 | Department Head | 2 business days |
| $5,000 – $24,999 | Department Head + VP of Finance | 3 business days |
| $25,000 – $99,999 | VP of Finance + CFO | 5 business days |
| $100,000+ | CFO + General Counsel + CEO | 10 business days |

**Additional rules:**
- Any vendor handling regulated data (PII, PHI, financial) requires Legal sign-off regardless of cost tier.
- Any vendor with a Compliance Reviewer **Block** verdict cannot be approved at any tier without written CFO + Legal exception.
- Multi-year commitments: advance to the next tier (e.g., a 3yr $18K contract counts as $54K → CFO tier).
- Renewals with <10% cost increase and existing Compliance Pass: Department Head approval only.

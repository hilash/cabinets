## Summary

HR requests Rippling (HRIS + payroll + benefits) to replace the current Gusto + Lattice split stack. Estimated annual cost: $38,000 for 210 employees. Requires DPA before provisioning (PII/payroll data). CFO + Legal approval required (>$25K threshold).

---

## Business Case (requester's words)

"We're running payroll on Gusto and performance reviews on Lattice with no integration between them. Every quarter we manually reconcile compensation changes and it takes 3 days. Rippling would unify both, automate payroll sync, and give managers a single view of their team's comp, benefits, and performance. ROI is the 3 days × quarterly = ~12 days/yr of ops time recovered, plus eliminating the reconciliation error risk."

Salesforce Native Two-Way Sync

Enable real-time bidirectional sync between our platform and Salesforce CRM — eliminating the weekly CSV export that costs Enterprise ops teams 4+ hours per week.

1 Problem

Enterprise and Mid-Market customers manage records in both our platform and Salesforce. Currently there is no native sync — customers export CSV every Friday and manually import. This costs 2–4 hours per ops person per week, creates 7-day data lag, and causes divergent records that break reporting.

Signal
38 feedback signals · $2.1M ARR requesting · $480K ARR at renewal risk. Northwind shipped a one-way connector 6 months ago — we are behind on table stakes.

2 Business Goal

Reduce customer data operations overhead by ≥80% for accounts using both platforms. Target: 0 accounts citing "no Salesforce sync" as a renewal risk by end of Q2 2026.

4 User Stories

US-01 · Real-time push sync
As an Enterprise ops manager, I want records updated in our platform to sync to Salesforce within 15 minutes, so that sales reps always see current account status without manual exports.
  • A record change appears in the linked Salesforce object within 15 minutes at P95.
  • If sync fails, an error is logged and an admin email is sent within 5 minutes.
US-02 · Bidirectional sync
As an Enterprise ops manager, I want Salesforce changes to sync back to our platform, so that Salesforce remains the system of record for account data.
  • A field change in Salesforce propagates to our platform within 15 minutes.
  • Conflict resolution: Salesforce wins on shared fields; our platform wins on exclusive fields.
US-03 · Per-field mapping control
As an Enterprise admin, I want to configure which fields sync in each direction, so that I don't overwrite fields managed exclusively in one system.
  • Field mapping UI allows per-field direction: → SF, ← SF, two-way, or excluded.
  • Mapping changes take effect within 5 minutes without a full re-sync.

# SaaS Startup

A product-led B2B SaaS company building developer tools. The company runs a freemium model with self-serve onboarding and usage-based expansion revenue.

## Why This Template

- Covers the classic SaaS operating model: engineering ships, growth acquires, customer success retains.
- Natural PLG loop where product usage drives upgrades.
- Clean separation between building the product, growing the pipeline, and keeping customers happy.

## Active Root Team

- CEO for company strategy, fundraising, and cross-team alignment
- CTO for architecture decisions, tech debt management, and engineering culture
- Head of Growth for acquisition funnels, activation experiments, and expansion revenue

## Child Cabinets

- `engineering` for sprints, architecture decisions, and release management
- `customer-success` for onboarding playbooks, churn prevention, and health scoring
- `growth` for acquisition channels, activation experiments, and conversion optimization

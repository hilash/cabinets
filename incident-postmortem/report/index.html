2 Root Cause

A NullPointerException in PaymentService.processCharge was triggered when the Stripe webhook client returned a response with a null charge.payment_intent field — introduced in Stripe's v4.1 library as a non-breaking schema change but not handled in our client code. The vulnerability existed because the payment service had no nil-guard on the payment-intent field and no circuit breaker to shed load when the error rate exceeded a threshold.

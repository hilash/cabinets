4 Gross Margin & Burn

Gross margin came in at 72.5%, down 0.4pp from March (72.9%), reflecting a modest infrastructure cost increase during the mobile API load testing period. We expect margin to recover to 73%+ in May as Datadog usage normalizes.

Net burn increased $37k MoM to $611k, driven by the two R&D sprint contractors ($42k/month above plan) and the Boston sales kickoff pull-forward ($3k one-time). Underlying burn excluding these items is $566k — in line with plan. The contractor engagement runs through June; expect elevated burn until a project scope decision is made.

5 Forward Look — May

May priorities: close the two professional services SOWs (target: +$80k services revenue), convert two enterprise pipeline deals (combined ACV $280k) to push ARR past $8.5M, and make a go/no-go decision on extending the mobile contractor engagement past June. If contracts extend and the enterprise deals slip, burn will remain elevated at $611–$640k and runway will decline below 15 months by June 30 — which would warrant a board-level conversation in Q3.

# Release Checklist

## Before Shipping

- Critical notification bugs reviewed
- Onboarding regressions checked
- Subscription flow sanity checked
- Rollback plan written
- Monitoring thresholds confirmed

## Ship Standard

If the app cannot be trusted to gently remind someone at the right time, it is not ready.

Ship — deprecate static banner.
The contextual modal delivers +2.9pp conversion lift (p=0.018) with no adverse guardrail movements. CI lower bound of +0.5pp means even the pessimistic case is positive. Effect is concentrated in early-tenure users — the highest-value conversion segment. Static banner will be deprecated in the next engineering sprint.

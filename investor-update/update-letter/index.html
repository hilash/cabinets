  • Series B process launched — we've had strong first meetings with Veritas Capital and Sequoia. Target LOI by May 30. The pitch centers on NRR + our AI differentiation in Meridian 2.0. I'll share the deck by end of this week.
  • Meridian 2.0 feature freeze hit May 28 — on track for GA on June 15. This is the AI workflow layer we've been building for 6 months. First look for investors at our June product demo day.

# Content Creator

A solo content creator running a multi-platform content operation. No child cabinets — this is a flat structure showing how a single person can use Cabinet to systematize their workflow.

## Why This Template

- Shows the solo operator pattern: no child cabinets, just agents that help one person do more.
- Demonstrates that cabinets work for individuals, not just companies.
- Clean workflow from ideation through publishing to performance review.

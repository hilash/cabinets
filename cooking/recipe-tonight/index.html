Sheet-pan salmon with lemon & herbs

uses · salmon (expires tomorrow) 10 min prep 18 min cook 2 servings ★★★★★ · last cooked Apr 12
Salmon is the most-expiring item in your fridge — needs to be cooked tonight or tomorrow. You haven't had fish in 14 days, and you have lemons + spinach + olive oil all ready. Mediterranean weeknight, 28 min total. Easy win.
Servings
2
scaled for 2
Ingredients
2 filletssalmon (≈ 200g each)expires tomorrow
1lemon, sliced thinhave 4
3 tbspolive oil, goodhave
2 clovesgarlic, mincedhave head
1 small bunchbasil + dill (chopped)basil exp Apr 30
200gbaby spinach (wilted under salmon)exp tomorrow
to tasteflaky sea salt + black pepperhave
Steps
1Heat oven to 220°C / 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.~5 min
2Lay spinach on the pan in a thin bed. Drizzle 1 tbsp olive oil + pinch salt.
3Pat salmon dry. Place skin-side down on spinach. Top with lemon slices, minced garlic, drizzle remaining olive oil. Generous salt + pepper.
4Bake 12–14 min until salmon flakes at thickest point but center is just translucent. The spinach beneath will steam-wilt.12–14 min
5Scatter chopped basil + dill over the top. Squeeze half a lemon. Serve with the wilted spinach.
6Optional: a hunk of sourdough to mop the pan juices. (You have one on the counter.)

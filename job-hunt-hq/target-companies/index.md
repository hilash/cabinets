# Target Companies

Research notes on companies in your pipeline. Know them before you apply, know them deeply before you interview.

## Company Research Template

For each target company, answer:

1. **What do they do?** — One sentence a non-tech friend would understand
2. **Business model** — How do they make money? Who pays?
3. **Stage and size** — Funding, headcount, growth trajectory
4. **Product org** — How big is the PM team? Who leads it? How do they work?
5. **Recent news** — Last 90 days: launches, fundraising, layoffs, leadership changes
6. **Why you** — What specific experience makes you a fit for this team?
7. **Red flags** — Glassdoor themes, turnover signals, org instability
8. **Connection** — Do you know anyone there? Who could intro you?

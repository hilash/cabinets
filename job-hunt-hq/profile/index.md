# Your Profile

This is home base. Everything the agents need to represent you starts here.

## What Lives Here

- **[[master-resume]]** — the full, un-trimmed version of your resume with every role, project, and achievement. This is the raw material that gets tailored per application.
- **[[skills-inventory]]** (CSV) — a structured list of your technical and soft skills with proficiency levels and years of experience. Agents use this to match you against job requirements.
- **[[target-roles]]** — what you're looking for: role titles, seniority, industries, location, comp range, and non-negotiables.

## How to Use This

1. Fill in the master resume first — be generous, include everything. The Resume Tailor will cut it down per job.
2. Be honest in the skills inventory. Inflating skills leads to interviews you can't pass.
3. Revisit target roles every two weeks. Your criteria will sharpen as you learn what the market actually values.

## Tailored Resumes

When you run the `resume-tailor-for-job` job, the output gets saved here as a new page — one per application. This way you always know exactly which version you sent to which company.

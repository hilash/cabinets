## Summary

A concise 2-3 sentence professional summary. Focus on what you do, who you do it for, and what makes you effective. Avoid buzzwords.

> Example: Product manager with 6 years of experience shipping B2B SaaS tools. Led cross-functional teams from discovery through launch at companies from Series A to public. Strongest in data-informed prioritization and turning ambiguous problems into shipped features.

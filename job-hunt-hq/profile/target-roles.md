# Target Roles

> Update this as your search sharpens. Be specific — vague criteria produce vague results.

## Primary Target

- **Title:** Senior Product Manager / Product Lead
- **Seniority:** Senior IC or first-time people manager
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS, developer tools, data platforms
- **Company stage:** Series B through pre-IPO (50-500 people)
- **Location:** Remote-first or San Francisco Bay Area
- **Comp range:** $180K-$240K total comp

## Secondary Target

- **Title:** Product Manager, Growth / Activation
- **Seniority:** Mid to Senior
- **Industry:** Consumer tech, fintech, edtech
- **Company stage:** Series A through Series C
- **Location:** Remote OK
- **Comp range:** $160K-$210K total comp

## Non-Negotiables

- Must have a real product org (not just "the CEO decides everything")
- Engineering team of at least 10 — smaller means no leverage for PM
- Clear path to impact within first 90 days
- No return-to-office mandates without flexibility

## Nice-to-Have

- Mission I can explain to my friends without their eyes glazing over
- Product-led growth motion (I'm strongest here)
- Manager who has managed PMs before (not eng lead doubling as PM manager)

## Anti-Targets (Do Not Apply)

- Agencies or consulting firms
- Pre-product startups looking for a PM to also be the designer and QA
- Companies where "PM" means project management
- Any role requiring 10+ years when the job is clearly mid-level

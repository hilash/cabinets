# Job Hunt HQ

Job search is a full-time job. Staff it.

You are not "applying to jobs." You are running a pipeline with sourcing, qualification, outreach, interviews, and negotiation. This cabinet gives you the team, the systems, and the tracking to treat it that way.

## How This Works

1. **Set up your profile** in [[profile]] — your master resume, skills inventory, and target roles
2. **Save jobs** to [[job-board]] as you discover them (or let the daily scan find them)
3. **Watch your pipeline** on the [[pipeline]] board as applications move through stages
4. **Prep for interviews** using [[interviews]] — STAR stories, company research, feedback logs
5. **Work your network** through [[networking]] — contacts, warm intros, outreach templates
6. **Track everything** on the [[dashboard]] — response rates, velocity, what's working

## Your Team

- **Career Strategist** — positioning, target selection, overall search strategy, interview prep (STAR stories, prep packets, debriefs), and networking outreach (warm paths, follow-up cadence)
- **Resume Tailor** — customizes your resume for each application using keywords and framing

## Jobs That Run

- `daily-job-scan` — surfaces new matching roles from your target criteria
- `weekly-pipeline-review` — health check on your full pipeline, flags stale applications
- `resume-tailor-for-job` — generates a tailored resume for a specific job posting
- `follow-up-nudge` — reminds you to follow up on applications and networking

# Application Pipeline

Your job search is a sales funnel. This board shows every opportunity by stage.

## Stages

1. **Saved** — Interesting roles you haven't applied to yet
2. **Applied** — Application submitted, waiting for response
3. **Screen** — Phone screen or recruiter call scheduled/completed
4. **Interview** — In the interview process (technical, onsite, panel)
5. **Offer** — Received an offer
6. **Accepted** — Signed and done
7. **Rejected** — They said no
8. **Ghosted** — No response after 3+ weeks

## Health Rules

- If something sits in "Applied" for 10+ days, follow up or flag it
- If you have fewer than 3 things in "Screen" or "Interview," you need more top-of-funnel
- If your rejection rate is above 80%, revisit your targeting or resume
- Ghosted is not rejection — sometimes following up resurfaces the opportunity

## Data

Pipeline data lives in `applications.csv`. The visual board reads from this file.

# Interview HQ

Every interview is a rep. Track them, prep for them, and learn from them.

## What Lives Here

- **Interview Tracker** (visual board) — see all upcoming and past interviews at a glance
- **[[star-stories]]** — your bank of STAR-format stories mapped to common competencies
- **Feedback Log** (CSV) — structured debrief after every interview conversation

## Before Every Interview

1. Research the company (check `/target-companies` and run a quick search)
2. Review the role and your tailored resume for it
3. Pick 3-5 STAR stories from the bank that match the likely interview focus
4. Know the interviewer's name, role, and anything you can find on LinkedIn
5. Prepare 2-3 thoughtful questions that show you've done your homework

## After Every Interview

Log it in the feedback CSV:
- What went well (be specific)
- What you'd do differently
- Any surprise questions to prep for next time
- Your gut read on the vibe (Strong / Warm / Neutral / Cold)
- Whether you sent a follow-up thank you

## Patterns to Watch

- If "could improve" keeps saying the same thing, that's a skill to drill
- If surprise questions cluster around a theme, build a story for it
- If vibe reads are consistently cold, revisit your energy and opening

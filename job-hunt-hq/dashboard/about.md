# Dashboard

The visual command center for your job search. Open the HTML board for the full view.

## Key Metrics to Watch

- **Response Rate** — what percentage of applications get any response? Below 20% means your targeting or resume needs work
- **Time to First Response** — how many days from application to first contact? Faster = better targeting
- **Interview Conversion** — what percentage of screens become full interviews? This measures your phone screen skills
- **Pipeline Velocity** — how many new opportunities enter the pipeline per week vs how many close?
- **Channel Performance** — which sources (referral, LinkedIn, career page) produce the best results?

## What Healthy Looks Like

| Metric | Struggling | Okay | Strong |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Response rate | Under 15% | 15-30% | 30%+ |
| Screen to interview | Under 30% | 30-50% | 50%+ |
| Applications per week | 1-2 | 3-5 | 5-8 targeted |
| Active pipeline | Under 3 | 3-8 | 8-15 |
| Referral % of pipeline | 0% | 20-40% | 40%+ |

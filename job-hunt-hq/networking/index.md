# Networking

Most jobs are filled through people, not portals. This is where you manage the human side of the search.

## What Lives Here

- **Contacts** (CSV) — every relevant person with warmth score, status, and last contact date
- **[[outreach-templates]]** — message templates for reconnects, referral asks, cold outreach, and follow-ups

## Warmth Scale

| Level | Meaning | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hot | Strong relationship, would vouch for you | Former manager, close colleague |
| Warm | Know each other, positive history | Conference contact, LinkedIn with real interaction |
| Cold | No relationship yet, need an introduction | 2nd degree, never met |

## Weekly Rhythm

- Send 3-5 personalized outreach messages per week (not 50 cold blasts)
- Follow up on any "Sent" contacts that haven't replied in 5+ days
- After every interview, check if the interviewer is worth adding to your network long-term
- Update the CSV after every interaction so the Networking Scout can help you

## The Math

- 1 warm referral = ~10 cold applications in terms of response rate
- Companies with an internal referral typically interview 50%+ of referred candidates
- Your network is your highest-ROI channel. Invest in it even when it feels awkward.

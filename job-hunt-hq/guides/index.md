## The Meta-Advice

- Your job search is a funnel. Treat it like one. Measure conversion rates, not just activity.
- Quality applications with warm intros beat volume every single time.
- Interview skills improve with reps. Your first few interviews should not be at your top-choice companies.
- Take notes after every interaction. Your future self will thank you.
- Set a weekly rhythm: apply Monday-Wednesday, network Thursday, review Friday.
- The emotional rollercoaster is real. Ghosting hurts. Rejections sting. One good offer makes it all worthwhile.

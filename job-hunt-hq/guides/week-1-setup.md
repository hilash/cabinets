# Week 1 Setup Guide

Before you send a single application, spend 3-5 days setting up your infrastructure. This is the difference between a chaotic spray-and-pray search and a focused, trackable operation.

## Day 1: Know Yourself

- [ ] Write your master resume in `/profile/master-resume.md` — include everything, hold nothing back
- [ ] Fill out the skills inventory in `/profile/skills-inventory.csv` — be honest about proficiency levels
- [ ] Define your target roles in `/profile/target-roles.md` — titles, industries, comp range, non-negotiables

**Gut check:** Can you explain in one sentence what you want and why you're good at it?

## Day 2: Know Your Market

- [ ] Pick 8-12 target companies and research them in `/target-companies`
- [ ] For each company, find at least one connection (1st or 2nd degree on LinkedIn)
- [ ] Read 3-5 job postings for your target title to understand common requirements

**Gut check:** Do your target roles match your actual experience, or are you reaching? Reaching is fine — know when you're doing it.

## Day 3: Set Up Your Pipeline

- [ ] Seed your pipeline with 5-8 opportunities in `/pipeline/applications.csv`
- [ ] Categorize them: 2-3 "safe" applications, 3-4 "target" applications, 1-2 "stretch" applications
- [ ] Identify which ones have a warm path via your network

**Gut check:** If every company said yes to an interview tomorrow, would you be excited about all of them?

## Day 4: Prepare Your Stories

- [ ] Write 4-5 STAR stories in `/interviews/star-stories.md`
- [ ] Tag each story by competency (leadership, data, conflict, growth, shipping)
- [ ] Practice telling each story out loud in under 2 minutes

**Gut check:** Can you tell each story without looking at your notes?

## Day 5: Activate Your Network

- [ ] Add 10-15 relevant contacts to `/networking/contacts.csv`
- [ ] Rate each by warmth (Hot / Warm / Cold)
- [ ] Send 3-5 warm outreach messages using templates from `/networking/outreach-templates.md`
- [ ] Set up LinkedIn job alerts for your target titles

**Gut check:** Did you ask for help from at least one person who would actually do it?

## After Week 1

You should have:
- A complete profile the agents can work with
- 5-8 opportunities in your pipeline
- 3-5 networking conversations started
- STAR stories ready for interviews
- A clear sense of where you're competitive and where you're stretching

Now start the rhythm: apply Monday-Wednesday, network Thursday, review Friday.

## Stage milestones

See `milestones.csv` (also surfaced on the [[survival-tracker/index|Survival Tracker]]). Newborn window covers:

- Lifts head briefly during tummy time (4–6 wk)
- Tracks objects with eyes (4–8 wk)
- Social smile (6–8 wk) — *the one that makes the rest of it worth it*
- Cooing sounds (6–8 wk)
- Brings hands to mouth (8–12 wk)
- Holds head steady unsupported (10–12 wk)

## Daily rhythm — newborn template

| Time | Block |
|---|---|
| Wake | Feed → diaper → tummy time (2–10 min) → wake window 60–90 min |
| Then | Nap 30–90 min |
| Repeat | 8–12 times until you collapse |
| Witching hour | 5pm–10pm — fussiest, most cluster-feeding |
| Cluster feeds | Often 4–8pm — the baby is loading the tank |

**Wake windows by week:**
- Weeks 0–4: 45–60 min
- Weeks 5–8: 60–75 min
- Weeks 9–12: 75–90 min

Going past wake window = overtired baby = harder to settle. Watch the clock more than the cues at this age.

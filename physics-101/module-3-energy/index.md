## Core Concepts

_TBD._ Minimum vocabulary to cover:

- **Energy** — definition, units (joules), the idea of "ability to do work"
- **Kinetic energy** — `KE = ½·m·v²`
- **Gravitational potential energy** — `PE = m·g·h`
- **Elastic potential energy** — `PE = ½·k·x²` (spring)
- **Work** — `W = F·d·cos(θ)`, the work-energy theorem
- **Conservation of energy** — total mechanical energy is constant in the absence of friction; friction converts mechanical energy to heat
- **Power** — `P = W/t`, units of watts

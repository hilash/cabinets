## Design Notes

**Connector pattern across all cabinets:**
- Gmail → outreach, follow-ups, notifications
- Google Calendar → scheduling, deadlines, reminders
- Google Sheets → tracking data, pipelines, budgets, CSVs
- GitHub → code-adjacent cabinets (ship-it-solo, open-source-maintainer, launch-week, indie-game-studio)

**What makes them import-ready:**
- Every agent has a specific role with a clear persona, not generic "assistant"
- Every job produces a concrete artifact (a brief, a report, a draft, a checklist)
- Content directories have seed data (CSVs, templates, example docs)
- Child cabinets break naturally along team/function boundaries

**Viral ranking (shareability x utility):**
- Tier 1 (share immediately): reply-to-everyone, ship-it-solo, wedding-ops, job-hunt-hq, parent-admin, thesis-survival
- Tier 2 (share with your niche): youtube-factory, podcast-machine, freelance-to-agency, launch-week, open-source-maintainer
- Tier 3 (solid utility, slower spread): the rest
